Hello!

My name is Ruth and I want to be a User Experience (UX) Designer.

I am looking to transition into a full time UX designer role after years as a front end developer

Why the change? After years of coding I find myself more interested in what the user has to say about the product. I enjoy helping others and I want to be in the front lines in creating the solution. I want to be part of the solution that connects humans to other humans through technology.

Experience

My background may not exactly match your list of qualifications, but I believe I have similar experiences that provide a good cornerstone to succeed as a UX designer. At multiple points in my career, I supported UX leads by observing user testing, creating wireframes and style guides, and developing clickable prototypes. I have worked with Business Analysts to translate requirements into visual design or wireframes.

I know have gaps in experience and in-depth knowledge of UX. So while I search for that full time role, I am working through Springboard's User Experience Design Learning Path to increase my knowledge. I am also documenting my journey into UX at RC+UX.

If you are interested in working together, let's connect.
