About

I thrive in a deadline-driven, technology-agnostic, and design focused team that operates in an Agile environment. I enjoy being involved on the first day of development and throughout the integration and QA phases of a site build. I enjoy working with application developers and visual artists often serving as the middle man between both worlds. My main focus is the presentation layer, but I am capable of filling in the user experience (UX) blanks as needed.

I truly enjoy helping others and I want to be in the front lines in designing the solution. I have a strong interest in creating an inclusive and barrier-free web and am an advocate for all users.
